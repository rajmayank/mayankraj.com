Hello, I'm Mayank Raj, a lead engine with Salesforce with expertise in designing and building systems for the major cloud providers - AWS, Azure, and GCP. My focus lies in the domains of machine learning/deep learning infrastructure, Big Data, and Web APIs. I have also explored exciting areas such as AR/VR and Voice technology. Today I work on improving the security standpoint of the Salesforce ecosystem of products by providing cryptography capabilities to the various teams.

My passion lies in developing high-performing products powered by AI/ML and Big Data. I specialize in designing scalable systems that are cost-effective to maintain, both in terms of human resources and infrastructure. For instance, I have designed orchestration systems that efficiently manage over 250TB of data. Additionally, I have created serverless architectures capable of handling around 100,000 long-running jobs, while ensuring sub-millisecond latency at the interfaces. When existing tools fall short, I have built modules from scratch, including a graph database engine capable of handling billions of nodes and edges.
